Welcome to Troop 128

We are Boy Scouts of America Troop 128 — dedicated to building leadership, character, and outdoor skills in young men.

Here you’ll find information about our meetings, camping trips, advancement opportunities, and other troop activities.

Our troop meets every Monday (when school is in session) at 7:00 PM at the church hall.

Upcoming Camping Trips

Outdoor adventures are a key part of scouting! Here are some of our upcoming camping trips and outings:

  • January 17–19: Winter Campout at Camp Shands
  • March 14–16: Canoe Trip at St. Mary’s River
  • May 9–11: Spring Court of Honor and Family Camp

Be sure to check your gear list and submit your permission slip before each event!
